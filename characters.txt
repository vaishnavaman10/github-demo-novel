Jay Catsby is Nick Carraway's new neighbor who lives in a palatial house in West Egg, where he frequently throws glamorous parties.

Nick, a man from Minnesota, is the narrator of The Great Catsby and the cousin of Daisy Buchanan.

Daisy Buchanan is the object of Jay Catsby's affection, Nick Carraway’s cousin, and Tom Buchanan’s wife.

Tom, a former football player and Yale graduate, marries Daisy Buchanan shortly after her family forbids her from being with Catsby.
